Color Recognition System

We can find your right cosmetic shade!

*We recommend you to take a picture under natural light for more accurate result.


Color Recognition system is to help you on finding the right cosmetic shade. This system will be available for any user who is interested in makeup. In order to maximize the accuracy of the result, it will suggest cosmetic products from the database. Then you could choose a product based on the preference and budget. Each product will have a page with product description, how to use and cruelty-free cosmetics label for the products that are have not tested on animals. After choosing the product, you may add the product to favorites and or to the shopping cart and proceed to checkout.

*Disclaimer: The picture you have uploaded might be saved in our database for the research purpose. The data will not be sold, rented, leased or forwarded to any third party. By uploading the image, you are agreeing to these terms.

Tips: The picture should be centered and taken in a place with a natural lighting to get more accurate results.
